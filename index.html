Parking Meter

Parking Meters can be found anywhere around Wellington. Hourly parking fees in the central business district range from $2.50 to $4.50 per hour, with a minimum charge of $1 for all transactions. They connect to networks which allow people to pay with debit/credit cards as well as snapper.




Satellite Dish

A satellite dish is a dish-shaped type of parabolic antenna usually found at the top of buildings or houses, they are designed to receive or transmit information by radio waves to or from a communication satellite.




Internet Router

A router is a networking device that forwards data packets between computer networks. They are used all over the world as routers perform the traffic directing functions on the Internet allowing us to have access to it. Data sent through the internet, such as a web page or email, is in the form of data packets.
