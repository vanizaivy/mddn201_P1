Wellington Council provides a range of services and facilities to assure easy and safe access to the city. Parking Meters can be found anywhere in the city and suburbs. Hourly parking fees in the central business district range from $2.50 to $4.50 per hour. Each parking meters connect to networks which allow people to pay with debit/credit cards as well as snapper.
